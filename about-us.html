How it all started?

A health scare in the founder’s family couple of years back jolted them. It was the time when his father needed constant care and frequent visits to the hospital for ECG check-ups to ensure quick recovery. However, this wasn’t easy, because his father lived in the other city. While we are provided with the devices to monitor blood pressure, they couldn’t find anything that focused on cardiac health particularly. Even after hunting through, for a portable ECG machine, it ultimately led to a dead-end. In need of a cost-effective easy-to-use device that would help an individual to keep check on their cardiovascular health at home with as accurate outcomes as to be trusted by the doctors drove them to find a reliable solution and invent one.

Know The Founder’s

Agatsa, the brainchild of Neha Rastogi and Rahul Rastogi, who rooted the brand in 2014 to break the stigma of hazardous health risks. There’s no secret sauce to success except a tough grind and experience. The two of them desired to build up their own brand after working their socks off, that could save lives. With a successful career spanning for more than a decade, Rahul Rastogi, the CEO and Founder of Agatsa, brings to the desk his years of experience and knowledge to conceptualize the disruptive product - Continuous Heart vital monitoring device - "SanketLife" which is a breakthrough in remote patient monitoring and wellness devices segment. He has also developed the schematic, PCB and industrial design of the product. He actively participates in making of the complex interpretation and algorithm of ECG and bringing new innovations to life. An ardent believer of Nothing is Impossible, Neha Rastogi, the COO of Agatsa has been one of the strongest pillars in laying the foundation of the company. A seasoned professional with more than 14 years of experience under her belt, she has built the way on her own to the top. She has also been listed in Forbes India W-Power: Self Made Women 2020. She is committed to building a brand that is dedicated to elevating consciousness and spreading awareness regarding cardiac care and healthy living.
